Our Hostel Provides the Following Facilities

🧹 Cleaning

Rooms and common areas are cleaned daily by trained staff to maintain hygiene and comfort.

📚 Library

A quiet and well-maintained library is available from 8 AM to 8 PM for focused study.

🍲 Mess (Veg Only)

Nutritious vegetarian meals served daily:
Breakfast: 8–10 AM
Lunch: 1–3 PM
Dinner: 7–9 PM

🧺 Laundry

Laundry services are available twice a week with washing and ironing facilities.

💰 Rent Information

  • 4-Seater Room: ₹5000 / month
  • 6-Seater Room: ₹4000 / month

Rent includes Wi-Fi, electricity, and water charges.
